Sign language — early prototype

Indian Sign Language phrasebook

Real-time ISL-to-text recognition needs a CNN trained on a large, labeled ISL video dataset, plus real-time pose tracking — that's a model-training project on its own, not something built inside a single chat session. What's below is a small phrasebook demo standing in for that future feature: tap a sign name to see and hear its translation, the way the finished feature would output text and speech once a real recognizer is trained.

Translation:

Roadmap for the real version: collect or license an ISL video dataset (e.g. INCLUDE or ISL-CSLRT), extract hand/pose landmarks per frame, train a CNN or CNN+LSTM classifier on landmark sequences, then run it live in the browser with TensorFlow.js or on-device MediaPipe so no video ever has to leave the phone.
